At Time Out Acres, our purpose is to teach humans and their dogs how to communicate effectively using the latest and most humane dog training strategies and tools. We utilize both positive reinforcement techniques, and reward-based training methods to build a relationship of trust and respect between you and your dog to establish long lasting results.

We are located just west of Omaha in the quite and lush area of Waterloo right next to Two Rivers State Park. An ideal environment for your puppy to be next to nature, relax, and focus on the training ahead.

01.

Expertise

The best way to get your dog off on the right paw is with our Board and Train Service.

This is the most effective solution for any problem from leash walking to advanced problem-solving for the most complicated behavioral issues. This service is offered on a case-by-case basis, so it is highly personalized to suit the specific needs of you and your dog. Currently we offer a two week training program that fixes most behavioral problems and will learn commands such as: sit, down, come, off, no, leave it, and more!

All task can be performed on and off leash and even in public spaces such as grocery stores or baseball games.

02.

Puppy

Our exclusive Puppy Training promotes gentle, playful training that will help your puppy develop a sound temperament and good manners.

We will get your puppy off to a strong, healthy start and address typical puppy issues. If you are unsure of which option is best for you and your pup, always feel free to reach out for a free consultation with our trainer to help determine best course of action.
